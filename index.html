Big Blind

A big blind normalizes values between stakes. The games shown here ranged from big blinds of $1 ($100 buy in) to big blinds of $50($5,000 buy in) with the majority of hands in the $6($600 buy in) - $10($1000 buy in) range. By using a measurement of big blind you are able to compare winnings across various games

For example if I win $1 in a game where the big blind is $1 and then $50 in a game where the big blind is $50, the graph will increase by 2 units.
